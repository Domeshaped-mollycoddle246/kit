## ⚙️ How to Use Kit

After installation, look for the Kit icon in your system tray or menu bar. Click the icon to open the main menu.

### Taking Screenshots
Select the Capture tool from the menu. You can choose to capture the entire screen or a specific part of the screen. Kit saves these files to your Pictures folder by default.

### Recording Audio
Select the Audio button to start a recording. Adjust your microphone settings if needed. Press Stop to save the file. The app tells you where it saved the file once the recording ends.

### Translating Text
Copy text from any document or website. Select the Translate option in the Kit menu. Paste the text into the box. Choose the language you want to use. Press the Translate button to see the result.

## 🛡 Privacy and Security

Kit keeps your files on your hard drive. Your screen captures and audio recordings stay private. The app does not transmit your personal data to remote servers. You control who sees your files.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a fast internet connection?
No. Kit functions without an internet connection. You download the app once and use it offline.

Does the app slow down my computer?
Kit runs in the background. It uses a small amount of memory to stay ready. Most users notice no change in computer speed.

Can I change where files save?
Yes. Open the settings menu inside the app. You can pick a new folder for your recordings and captures.

How do I remove the app?
Open your Control Panel. Choose Uninstall a Program. Select Kit from the list and follow the prompts to remove it.
